What is Cynergy?

Cynergy is the ﬁrst of its kind coding club at RUAS. The purpose of Cynergy is embodied in its core open principles - Collaboration, Innovation and Competition. Cynergy is a platform to promote learning and development of programming skills. It empowers students with competitive programming skills through activities like Hackathon and Share sessions. It shall serve as a platform for students to nurture and showcase their talent. The spirit of competition in group endeavours will sharpen analytical skills and facilitate peer-to-peer learning. Cynergy aims to promote a coding culture in RUAS, imbibing the art of programming in all its students

Objectives

    • To create and promote a healthy coding culture at RUAS through regular peer interactions
    • To promote development of students in competitive ﬁeld of application programming by conducting competitions
    • To encourage participation in hackathons and related competitive events across the globe
    • To conduct seminars and talks by experts in all domains of computer programming
    • To share practical insights in use of cutting-edge tools and technologies of the computing industry through tool and technology workshops
